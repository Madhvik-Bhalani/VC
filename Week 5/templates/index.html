Predict Diabetes in Seconds!

In this project, i tackled a binary classification problem aiming to predict the likelihood of an individual developing diabetes. Leveraging machine learning techniques, I explored various classification models including Logistic Regression, Decision Trees, Random Forests, and Support Vector Machine(SVM), among others. Extensive data preprocessing, feature engineering, and hyperparameter tuning were conducted to optimize model performance and predictive accuracy.
